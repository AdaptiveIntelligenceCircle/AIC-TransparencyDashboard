# Security Policy - AIC-TransparencyDashboard

The Transparency Dashboard exists to prove that true independence is possible through radical openness.

### Responsible Disclosure
Security issues should be reported privately.

**How to report:**
- Email: `security@adaptiveintelligencecircle.org`
- Do **NOT** open public issues.

We will acknowledge reports within 7 days.

### Our Commitments
- No hidden logs or backdoors.
- All audit data must be publicly verifiable.
- The dashboard itself must not become a point of centralization.
- Ethical metrics and governance are monitored in real-time.

During the Founder’s military service (2027–2029), security updates will be handled by trusted maintainers.

**Scope**: This policy covers the entire AIC-TransparencyDashboard repository.
