# Transparency Policy - AIC-TransparencyDashboard

**Official Transparency & Governance Dashboard** of Adaptive Intelligence Circle & Human Meaning Network

### 1. Vision
Radical transparency is not optional — it is the foundation of trust and the strongest defense of the third path.

This dashboard exists to make every major decision, policy change, and ethical compliance publicly auditable in real time.

### 2. Core Transparency Principles (Non-Negotiable)

- **Everything Public by Default**: All governance decisions, code changes, ethical metrics, and announcements must be visible to the public.
- **No Hidden Operations**: There shall be no secret backdoors, undisclosed partnerships, or private governance channels.
- **Real-Time Auditability**: All actions on core repositories and policies are logged and verifiable.
- **Ethical Metrics**: The dashboard must publicly display adherence to zero-donation, third path absolute, and ethical-from-kernel at all times.
- **Third Path Absolute**: The dashboard itself must remain independent from any government or corporate influence.

### 3. What Must Be Transparent
- All Pull Requests and code changes on core repositories
- Governance decisions and policy updates
- Ethical compliance status
- Contributor and maintainer activity (when relevant)
- Any external inquiry or pressure received by the project

### 4. Prohibited Actions
- Hiding or delaying public information
- Creating private channels for sensitive decisions
- Any attempt to reduce visibility of the project’s operations

### 5. Compliance
Any fork or derivative of the Transparency Dashboard must uphold this policy in full.  
Violation will be publicly documented.
